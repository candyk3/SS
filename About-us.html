Hey, Welcome to Serenity Sphere


In the contemporary digital age, mental health has become a pressing concern, with individuals facing various stressors in their day-to-day lives. Serenity Sphere is a pioneering project designed to provide users with an innovative platform for monitoring and enhancing their mental well-being. The core functionality of Serenity Sphere revolves around two key components: sentiment analysis of journal entries and evaluation of facial expressions. These journal entries are subjected to sentiment analysis algorithms, which provide insights into the user's emotional state and overall mental health. Additionally, Serenity Sphere utilizes facial recognition technology to evaluate the user's facial expressions, further enhancing the accuracy of mental health assessment.
